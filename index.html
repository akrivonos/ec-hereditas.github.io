About

EC Hereditas (short for European Center for Vernacular Spiritual Heritage Studies) is a research‚Äëdriven organization created by scholars of European demonology, folk beliefs and folk magic. We publish articles, curate open corpora, and carry out ethnographic expeditions.

Mission

To document, analyze, and compare Europe's vernacular spiritual heritage, and to support its preservation as intangible cultural heritage.

Approach

We bridge comparative ethnology, anthropology of religion, folklore studies, and field research, grounded in both the theoretical and empirical achievements of world anthropology.

Research

The Carpathian region serves as our starting field laboratory, offering a lens for broader European comparison.

  • Folklore, Mythology, and Narrative Worlds
  • Songs, Ritual Poetry, and Performance
  • Material Expressions of Spirituality
